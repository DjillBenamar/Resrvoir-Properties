  • Pc [psia]

  • Tc [°R]

  • Tb [°R]

  • Vc [ft3/lb]

  • Zc

The accuracy of these results depends on the accuracy of: "Riazi and Daubert" equation, molecular weight and specific gravity of each fraction.
